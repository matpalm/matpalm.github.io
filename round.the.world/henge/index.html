so what is a henge?

a henge is a circular enclosure used for ceremonial purposes. to better explain what it is here is........

mats step by step instructions to make a henge


1. find a bit of ground, preferrably flat, that already hasn't got a henge on it.


2. dig a large circular ditch.


3. using the dirt you just dug out build a mound.


4. make a few ways in and well done! you have made a henge!!!!


5. and if you're really keen you can put a monument in there too! in the olden days they would use stone or wood but, obviously since they did this 4000 years ago, there are no wood henge monuments left.
